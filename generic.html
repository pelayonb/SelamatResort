Front Desk

Main duty is to enhance guest service by developing services to meet guests needs, and by providing information and services. The function of the front office is to sell rooms, maintain and balance accounts, and offer services, such as fax and mail, to guests.

Sales

Aim to convince customers that Selamat Resorts is the best destination to hold their events, company retreats, weddings, etc. and also convince that our resort will have the best customer service, amenities, and and the best vacation experience.

General Manager

The general manager supervises lower-level managers who are in charge of smaller divisions. Some of his/her duties include hiring, coaching, and disciplining of lower-level managers. The manger generates strategic plans for the business based on company goals. General Managers also work with upper level corporate workers to help budget for supplies, marketing, and hiring.
