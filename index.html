History

Selamat Resorts was founded by the partnership group of LNJC LLC in 2005. The first resort was founded in the heart of Bali, Indonesia. It has quickly grown and expanded into three international resorts, the other two located in the Las Vegas and the other in London, with other new locations to come. Selamat Resorts works to keep each resort expereince to be fresh and a unique experience. Each of our resorts have different themes that will engage the guest with a sight to behold. We are also a company that embodies the princlple of putting our employees first. We understand that if our employee is in top condition, then our resort will be in its best shape. Selamat resorts will continue to strive for the best and only for the best.
